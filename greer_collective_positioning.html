Positioning Statement

Jacqueline Greer is an executive coach and leadership consultant with 20+ years building and leading high-performing teams inside national education equity organizations. She has served as a Founding Executive Director, Chief Program Officer, and VP of Programs — raising millions in federal and philanthropic funding, leading large departments through organizational complexity, and delivering results even in the most dysfunctional environments. She holds a B.A. from the University of Chicago and a Master's degree from Harvard. Today, through Greer Collective, she brings that hard-won expertise directly to the leaders who need it most.

"I don't just teach leadership — I've lived it at every level. I know what it costs to lead well in rooms that weren't built for you, and I know how to help you do it anyway."
